Stay on the go.

A clean and minimilastic design lets the user focus on what is most important, where they want to go. Plan events and activities with your friends, family and even people you just met. Goer allows you to collect and organize all the events and plans you make in one place.

Experience

Users can choose which events they want to see in their feed first but swiping between three filters. Events can be synced to calendars both inide the app itself and through 3rd party applications. Creating events can either be done publically or privately for just you and your closest friends.

EVENT FEEDS

When making plans, choose from one of the three feeds GOER has to offer. The default feed shows posts in chronological order with the most recent being listed first. the “Live” feed shows events or activities that are going on now or will happen within the next 24 hours. the “GO” feed shows events in chornological order that the user haa shown intersted in attending when selecting the GO button on each post.

PROFILES

Users can personalize profiles with a photo, a description of their interest, and an external link to promote something such as a business or favorite hobby.

CALENDAR

Save events to your the in app Calendar and view them by month, day and list view as well as sync them to other 3rd party calendars.

NOTIFICATIONS

User will be notified when they recieve invitations, personal messages, RSVPs to their events as well as updates on what their friends and people that they follow are planning.

STOREFRONTS

Each event post has a storefront attached to it that houses everything that someone would need to know about that specific event or activity. The creator of the post can as a minimum add a brief summary, address, phone number, additional images, an external website link and 3rd party features such as ticket links, merchandise or pop up shops, music and more. The opportunity to monotize this with partners like ticket vendors, ride sharing companies and other brands for things such as payment sharing.

FUTURE POTENTIAL PARTNERS
Create

Creating an event is simple. Start by adding an image, a proffesionally designed flyer or invitation or just a meanigful photo, an event headline or title and a description. This is not only a great way for people to make simple plans like a work happy hour or surpirse birthday but bigger brands and celebrities can announce concert tours, retail sales and sporting events. Users also have complete control over who sees this event post by either making it public or private.
